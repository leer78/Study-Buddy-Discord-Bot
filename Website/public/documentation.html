    +eyes

    - Reminds you to look away from your screen every 20 minutes, following the 20-20-20 rule for eye strain.

    - Type the command again to cancel it
    +pomodoro

    - Method designede to increase productivity and manage your time. It involves work periods of 25 minutes each, followed by a 5 minute break. Every fifth work period is 30 minutes.

    - Type the command again to cancel it
    +todoAdd

    - Add reminders, chores, or important notes to a list

    - For example, +todoAdd homework will add homework to your personalized list
    +todoView

    - View reminders, chores, or important notes from your personalized list
    +todoDelete

    - Remove items from your personalized list

    - For example, +todoDelete 1 will remove the first item in your list
    +gaming

    - Updates your status to gaming mode

    - Frequent reminders to take breaks and stretch

    - Type the command again to cancel it
    +water

    - Sends hourly reminders to stay hydrated

    - Type the command again to cancel it


    Everyone loves playing video games, but sometimes we can get a little carried away. To ensure you are staying healthy while having fun, the Study Buddy Bot allows you to start gaming sessions. The session begins by typing the command +gaming, and will send you a direct message every hour reminding you to take a break from the games. When you are done playing, simply re-type the command +gaming to end your session. For more info about the importance of taking breaks while gaming, check out this Acer article.
